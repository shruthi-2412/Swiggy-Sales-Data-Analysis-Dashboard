# Swiggy Sales Data Analysis Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI** business intelligence project designed to analyze food delivery sales, customer purchasing behavior, restaurant performance, and revenue trends. This dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful, actionable business insights, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven decisions through dynamic visualizations.

## 📊 Business Insights & Features

*   **Executive KPI Tracking:** Real-time visibility into **Total Sales, Total Orders, Average Order Value (AOV), and Total Customers**.
*   **Dynamic Data Filtering:** Interactive slicers allow users to slice and dice data seamlessly by **City, Restaurant, Cuisine, Food Category, and Order Date**.
*   **Advanced Trend Analysis:** Utilizes **Line Charts, Treemaps, Ribbon Charts, Scatter Charts, Waterfall Charts, and Bar Charts** to break down performance.
*   **Operational Optimization:** Pinpoints top-performing restaurants, gauges seasonal demand peaks, and tracks category-wise preferences to improve overall customer satisfaction.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

*   **Microsoft Power BI** – For dashboard engineering and report authoring.
*   **Power Query** – For data extraction, cleaning, transformation, and loading (ETL).
*   **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – For writing advanced custom measures and calculated columns.
*   **Data Modeling** – Creating relational star/snowflake schemas for optimal performance.
*   **Data Visualization** – Designing user-centric UI/UX and interactive charts.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Visualizations Breakdowns

*   **Sales & Revenue Trends:** Spotting seasonal demand and growth trajectories over time.
*   **Restaurant Performance Evaluation:** Identifying high-revenue partners and service anomalies.
*   **Customer Behavior:** Mapping out cuisine preferences and regional purchasing patterns across different cities.
